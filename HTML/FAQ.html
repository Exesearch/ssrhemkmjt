FAQ



I'm lost! Please help.

If you are lost, load up Google maps. If you need immediate help, phone the campus Estate Patrol on 01392 723999, or if it's extremely serious, please call 999.

What is ExeSearch?

ExeSearch is a treasure hunt game designed to help new students find their way around campus and meet new people. Students are given clues to follow and tasks to complete in order to find their way to the final treasure.

How do I play the game?

Head to the quiz page. This is where you will be set all the challenges you'll need to complete to reach the end of the treasure hunt. You will be asked a question, and once you have the correct answer, type it in box and click "submit". After that, you will be given a clue leading you to a location on campus. You will need to walk to that location, then click the button to check your position. If you are in the right location, you will be given a task to do. This repeats until you have completed the treasure hunt.
