What on Earth is ExeSearch?

ExeSearch is a treasure hunt game designed to help you find their way around campus and meet new people! Receive clues to follow and tasks to complete in order to find your way to some special treasure! Neat, huh?

How do I play the game?

Once you log in, you will be told what group you are in and any ongoing games. Meet up with everybody on your team, hit "Ready", and then you'll be able to start! You can also see current progress on a game by clicking "View progress".

Once you've started a game, you'll be set all the challenges you'll need to complete to reach the end of the treasure hunt. Answer the question correctly, and you'll be given a clue leading you to a location on campus. You will need to walk to that location, then click the button to check your position. If you are in the right location, you will be given a task to do. This repeats until you have completed the game.
